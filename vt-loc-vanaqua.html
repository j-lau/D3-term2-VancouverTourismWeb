Vancouver Aquarium

845 Avison Way
Stanley Park
Vancouver BC
V6B 3X8

Phone: 604.659.3474
Fax: 604.659.3515
Visit Website | Send Email

Belugas, penguins, sea otters & 50,000 other aquatic creatures await your arrival at Canada's largest aquarium. Enjoy dolphin & beluga whale shows, sea otter feeds, shark dives & the free-roaming animals found in the popular Amazon Gallery. You can also immerse yourself in the 4D Experience Theatre, which combines the high-definition excitement of a 3D film with thrilling sensory effects!

Come find out for yourself why the Vancouver Aquarium has been voted one of the world's favourite aquariums by TripAdvisor (Travellers' Choice 2014). Located minutes from downtown in world famous Stanley Park.

Visitors to the Vancouver Aquarium can participate in our personalized, hands-on Animal Encounters including training and feeding the beluga whales, sea otters, dolphins, penguins and Steller sea lions. Animal Encounters must be booked in advance of your visit.

The Vancouver Aquarium Marine Science Centre is a self-supporting non-profit dedicated to effecting the conservation of aquatic life through display and interpretation, education, research and direct action. Through its visionary programs and the hard work of its staff and volunteers, the Vancouver Aquarium is Canadaâ€™s largest and one of the five largest in North America.

Long-established as a global leader in marine research, education and conservation, the Aquarium offers visitors an unforgettable experience through face-to-face engagement with a wide array of amazing animals.

The Vancouver Aquarium is open 365 days a year from 10:00 am - 5:00 pm (with extended summer hours). Telephone 604-659-FISH or dive in to vanaqua.org to learn more!
