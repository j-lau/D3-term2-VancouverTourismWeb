Vancouver Art Gallery

750 Hornby Street
Vancouver BC
V6Z 2H7

Phone: 604.662.4700
Fax: 604.682.1086
Visit Website | Send Email

A world-class art encounter waits at the Vancouver Art Gallery. Located in a stunning, turn-of-the century heritage building in the heart of downtown Vancouver; the Gallery showcases four floors of inspirational art pleasures.

2015 Highlights include: Emily Carr in Dialogue, Unscrolled: Reframing Tradition in Chinese Contemporary Art, Cézanne and the Modern: Masterpieces of European Art from the Pearlman Collection and Of Heaven and Earth: 500 Years of Italian Painting, and Geoffrey Farmer: How do I fit this ghost in my mouth?

Be sure to leave extra time to rest your feet at the city-famous Gallery Café and to peruse the Gallery’s Store for fantastic gift ideas. vanartgallery.bc.ca for more details.

Closed April 20th, December 25th and December 31st, 2015.
