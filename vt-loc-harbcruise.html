Harbour Cruises & Events

501 Denman Street
Vancouver BC
V6G 2W9

Phone: 604.688.7246
Fax: 604.687.5868
Visit Website | Send Email

Vancouver's best sightseeing, dining and special event cruises. Join Vancouver's most popular Sunset Dinner Cruise, an amazing opportunity to enjoy the fabulous sunset view with your family and special friends, also this is a great gift for your loved ones. Enjoy Vancouver's only authentic paddle boat and narrated tour of the inner harbour or relax with Indian Arm Luncheon Cruise which is a unique experience since the area is only accessible by boat, in 4 hours you will pass Deep Cove to Indian Arm and finally reach Silver Falls. Special event cruises throughout the year include the annual Fireworks Cruises in summer, Christmas Carol Ships Dinner & Dance Cruises and New Year's Eve Party Cruises in December.
